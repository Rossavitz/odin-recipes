Braised Short Ribs

Classic French Braised Short Ribs

Braised Short Ribs are a long slow cooked beef in red wine, mirepoix and herbs.

    Ingredients:
  • Short Ribs
  • Celery, onions, carrots
  • Red wine
  • Rosemary and Thyme
    Steps to make:
  1. Sautee mirepoix
  2. Add red wine and reduce
  3. Add Rosemary and Thyme
  4. Add short ribs
  5. Cover and cook in 350 over for 3 hours
